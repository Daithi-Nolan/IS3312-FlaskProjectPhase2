Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 6th December 2024

Welcome to WeatherWay (“we,” “our,” or “us”). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website http://127.0.0.1:5000/.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information to provide a better user experience and ensure the functionality of our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, billing and shipping addresses, payment information, and phone numbers.
  • Non-Personal Information: IP addresses, browser type, device details, referring URLs, and time spent on our website.

We may also collect information through cookies, analytics tools, and user feedback forms to enhance our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for various purposes, including:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders efficiently.
  • Improving the functionality and usability of our website.
  • Providing customer support and addressing your inquiries.
  • Sending promotional emails and newsletters (if you have opted in).
  • Ensuring the security of transactions and preventing fraud.

3. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted third-party service providers for specific purposes:

  • Payment processing services (e.g., payment gateways).
  • Shipping and logistics partners to deliver your orders.
  • Marketing tools to improve user engagement.
  • Legal authorities if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Recognize and remember your preferences.
  • Analyze website traffic and improve our offerings.
  • Deliver personalized content and ads based on your interests.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect certain website functionalities.

5. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure server technologies. However, no system is entirely foolproof, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional communications at any time.
  • Request details about how your information is used.

To exercise these rights, contact us at admin@weatherway.com.

7. Children's Privacy

WeatherWay is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective upon posting, and the updated policy will include the "Effective Date" at the top of the page. Please review this policy periodically for any updates.
